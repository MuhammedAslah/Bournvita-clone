BOURNVITA

Introduced: 1948

Cadbury Bournvita malted drink debuted in 1948 in India, the same year Cadbury India was established. In India, Cadbury Bournvita is one of India's most trusted brands and comes in both a chocolate-and-caramel flavor called Cadbury Bournvita 5 Star Magic. In Nigeria, Cadbury Bournvita is known as “the food drink for vitality” and is endorsed by the Nutrition Society of Nigeria.
